Scientific Name:

Blenderman

Discovered by / at:

Discovered by: Unknown. Urban legend known to visit people in their nightmares. Has appeared throughout history but first appearance is also unknown.

Family:

This creature belongs to the family of Fears. All those fears that bother and constantly affect us.

Habitat:

This creature lives in the nightmarescape and thrives off of fear. That can mean different environments for different people. For a majority of people there is a fear associated with the woods and darkness. This specific creature likes to run freely throughout the forest.

Appearance:

This creature is a tall dark silhouette creature. He is a combination of a slenderman and a sleep paralysis demon. He has long legs and talon like arms.

Key Characteristics:

  • Violently attacks when interacted with
  • Lives in an environment created by your fears
  • Feeds off of fear

What we know so far about Blenderman? Blenderman is a tall black humanoid creature that lives in the nightmare scape. His environment is determined by what the person he is haunting associates with fear. He is usually violent and will attack anyone that interacts with him but he can be hurt. His origin is unknown but he is said to haunt the dreams of stressed individuals. The earliest recorded encounter is 1664 by A Dutch physician.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature The creature follows the the user when the mouse is on the canvas. When the mouse is pressed the creature will run away from the mouse. When the k key is pressed the cursor turns into an axe at which point the killing mode is activated. In the killing mode the creature can be hurst when the mouse is pressed and over the head of the creature.
